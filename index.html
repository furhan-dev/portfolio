About Me

Hi! I'm Furhan Samani! I was born in Dubai and have lived in North America since the age of 5 when my family decided to immigrate to the United States. When I was 14, we once again moved countries, this time to Canada where I attended High School and University. I currently reside in Kirkland, WA, where I've been fortunate to call home for the past 12 years.

Professionally, I'm a Software Engineer with years of experience building infrastructure tools and writing robust automation. I'm currently learning the MERN stack via small and large projects which I'll add to the 'My Projects' section below.

In my spare time I like to play a ton of pickleball and spoil my beautiful niece, Sienna, whenver I can. I also enjoy travel and will soon add a blog section to keep you updated on my adventures throughout the world! My other hobbies include hiking, biking, stand up paddleboarding, and playing video games with friends.
